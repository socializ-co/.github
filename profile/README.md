# Socializ

Open-source, self-hosted social platform for individuals and communities.

Socializ helps people and organizations run their own social infrastructure with full control over their data.

## What is Socializ?

Socializ is built with:

- TypeScript
- NestJS
- Nx
- PostgreSQL
- Docker

## Editions

### Community Edition

For individuals and personal self-hosted deployments.

The Community Edition is open source and licensed under Apache-2.0.

### Enterprise Edition

For organizations that need:

- Organizations and teams
- RBAC
- SSO
- Audit logs
- Billing
- Enterprise support

Enterprise features are maintained separately.
